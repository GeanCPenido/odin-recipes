Cassava Puffs

Description

Cassava puffs are a traditional brazilian dish made of tapioca flour and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 250ml of milk;
  • 250ml of oil;
  • 250ml of water;
  • 1 spoon of salt;
  • 500g of tapioca flour;
  • 4 eggs;
  • Grated cheese.

Steps

  1. Boil the milk, water, oil and salt together;
  2. In a large bowl scald the tapioca flour with the boiled liquid and mix them with a spoon until it cool;
  3. When it cooldown mix it with the grated cheese and the eggs with your hands;
  4. Anoint your hands with oil and make 200mg balls with your hands out of the mass;
  5. Cook in the oven at 230Â°C for 45min.
