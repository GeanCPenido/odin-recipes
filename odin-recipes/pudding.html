Pudding

Description

Sweet pie made of condensed milk and eggs.

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 1 condensed milk;
  • 250ml of milk;
  • 3 eggs.

Syrup

  • Sugar;
  • Water.

Steps

Syrup

  1. In a pan cook enough sugar until it starts to melt;
  2. As the sugar melts add a little bit of water to form a liquid;
  3. Grease the pudding mold with the syrup and reserve it.

Pastry

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a blender for 2min and dump it on the mold;
  2. Cook it on bain marie in the oven at 250ÂºC for 1hr.
