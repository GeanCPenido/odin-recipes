Cassava Puffs

Description

Cassava puffs are a traditional brazilian dish made of tapioca flour and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk;
  • 1 cup of water;
  • 1/3 cup of oil;
  • 1 spoon of salt;
  • 500g tapioca flour;
  • grated cheese;
  • 3 eggs.

Steps

  1. Boil the milk with the water and the oil;
  2. In a bowl mix the tapioca flour and the salt;
  3. Put the boiled liquid with the flour and mix with a large spoon;
  4. Wait cooldown and mix in the grated cheese and the eggs;
  5. Mix the pasta with your hands;
  6. Make round puffs at the size you want;
  7. Bake in the oven.
