Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a traditional brazilian chocolate sweet in spherical form.

Ingredients

  • 1 condensed milk;
  • 1 spoon of margarine;
  • 7 spoons of milk chocolate;
  • Chocolate sprinkles.

Steps

  1. Mix the condensed milk, margarine and the chocolate milk in a deep pan;
  2. Cook in medium-heat until it unglued from the pan;
  3. Let it cool, make small balls and cover it up with the sprinkles.
