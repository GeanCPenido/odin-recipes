Pudding

Description

Sweet pie made of condensed milk.

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 1 condensed milk;
  • 1 cup of milk;
  • 4 eggs.

Syrup

  • 1 cup of sugar;
  • 1/3 cup of water.

Steps

Syrup

  1. In a pan mix the water and the sugar until it forms the syrup;
  2. Grease a pudding mold with the syrup and reserve it.

Pastry

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a blender and dump it on the mold;
  2. Cook it in bain marie for 40min.
